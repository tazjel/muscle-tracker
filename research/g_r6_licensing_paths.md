# G-R6: Commercial Licensing Paths for SMPL + Texture Models

| Component | License | Commercial Path |
|---|---|---|
| SMPL Model | Non-commercial (MPI) | Requires commercial license from **Meshcapade**. |
| FLUX.1-dev | Non-commercial | Switch to **FLUX.1-schnell** (Apache 2.0) and retrain LoRAs. |
| SD 1.5 | CreativeML OpenRAIL-M | Commercial OK with restrictions. |
| GHUM / STAR | Varies | GHUM (Google) is restrictive; STAR is an alternative topology but may have similar origin issues. |

### Recommendation:
For a  pure commercial launch, the main blocker is the SMPL topology. Contact Meshcapade for startup tier licensing.
