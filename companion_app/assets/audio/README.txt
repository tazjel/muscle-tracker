Audio assets for GTD3D Full Body Scan

Required files (MP3 format):
- pass1_start.mp3  — "Stand 2.5 meters from camera. Begin rotating slowly."
- pass2_start.mp3  — "Move to 1 meter. Begin rotating."
- pass3_start.mp3  — "Move to half a meter. Slow rotation for skin detail."
- rotate_cue.mp3   — Short beep/tick (plays every ~2.5 seconds during rotation)
- pass_complete.mp3 — "Pass complete. Move closer."
- scan_complete.mp3 — "Scan complete. Uploading."

For initial development, the app uses system beep tones as fallback
when these files are not present.
