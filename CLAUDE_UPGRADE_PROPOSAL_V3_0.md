Following strategic review, the priority for v3.0 has shifted. While v2.0 provided the metrology core, we are now entering the **"Second Level"** of development. ## 2. Phase 1: Advanced Research & Metrology (P0)
**Objective**: Transition from heuristic 2D models to SOTA 3D volumetric analysis.

### 2.1 — 3D Gaussian Splatting (3DGS) Integration
- **Task 1.1**: Research **Gaussian Grouping** and **ReferSplat** (2024-2025) to allow "segmentation-within-3DGS". This will enable clinicians to isolate a single muscle (e.g., the bicep) from a full 3D limb scan.
- **Task 1.2**: Prototype a **Gaussian Splat Generator** that creates high-fidelity 3D geometry from Front/Side/Side-Angle photos, bypassing the limitations of simple elliptical models.

### 2.2 — Foundation Model Segmentation (SAM 3D / nnSAM2)
- **Task 2.1**: Implement **Segment Anything Model (SAM) 3D** for training-free muscle ROI extraction. This eliminates the need for manual templates and handles occlusions more effectively.
- **Task 2.2**: Review **nnSAM2** (2025 research) for few-shot multi-modality segmentation, improving accuracy on lower-resolution mobile camera captures.

### 2.3 — Training-Free Volumetric Math
- **Task 3.1**: Incorporate **optical flow-based keypoint tracking** (2025 research) to measure muscle deformation during contraction.
- **Task 3.2**: Replace basic volumetric math with **Segmental Volume Profiling**, calculating cross-sectional areas at 1mm intervals for a "continuous" volume profile.
